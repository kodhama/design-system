# divergent-researcher — design-system addendum

## Research artifacts live under specs/; no research rubric

design-system has no separate research-artifact directory and no dedicated
research-quality rubric (`RESEARCH_RUBRIC_PATH` is honest-none in
`.grove/config.toml`). File research artifacts under `specs/` (`type:
discovery`). Where the charter says "self-check against the research-quality
rubric," self-check against `specs/README.md`'s artifact contract instead:
every load-bearing claim tagged, no claim resting on recall alone. Only then
promote `draft → gated`.
