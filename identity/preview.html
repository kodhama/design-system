Family rule: spirits are solid, structure is faint. kodhama and wisp carry the living spark (solid fills); grove and trellis are the structure the spirits inhabit — no solid fill, depth comes from the two-tone --accent-soft strand instead of opacity. Org: kodhama — a play on the kodama tree spirits (Mononoke Hime): the forest all of this lives in.
